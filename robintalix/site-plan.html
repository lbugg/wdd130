Overview

Purpose

My friends and I play a lot of Pathfinder 2nd Edition together. I have a lot more experience with the system than they do, and I’d like to make a website that will explain how my character works, how he was made, and how he plays. Have you ever felt dumb because you were introduced to a new game or program that everyone else knew but you didn’t? I want to make this website so that my friends, and others who are new to the system, can easily learn how things work to help them with their own characters without feeling dumb because of all the questions they have.

Audience

My friends, as well as others who want to learn and better understand the system, should be able to read through the information I post about my character to help them build and play their own. The hope is that through explaining the choices I made with the build, and the mechanics behind how he actually functions, they can get a better handle on how the game works and how characters builds are meant to flow.
